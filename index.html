Home

Welcome to One Way Photo, a small but humble gallery where I showcase photographs that captures the pure tranquillity within pieces. This rather small showcase is for you to enjoy whether you want to just have a look or want to have a unique piece for yourself. The collection mainly focusses on Travel photography, with it I want to show that if you focus and look closely there is a lot of beauty and peace still left in the world, but you have to pay attention. So get your one way ticket and travel with me.

About

Hi, I'm Lonneke! Your casual photographing travelnerd. I've always been making photos, but my real passion for it started when I started travelling. The views and special moments were the trigger, my dad lend me his old Olympus camera and that's where it all started. Before I knew it I was in a camera shop buying my own, new Olympus camera. I believe making a photograph is an art form that can't be cheated on, you'll always capture the pure essence of someone or something. It all depends on how you as the photographer feels that day, how the day sets and how the object or person you're photographing reacts.
