Welcome

Welcome to Shashin, a small but humble photo gallery with beautiful photos made from all over the world. With this gallery I want to show that if you focus and look closely there is a lot of beauty and peace left in the world, but you have to pay attention. These unique pieces are for you to enjoy whether that's here on this website or on your own wall at home. Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions and most of all enjoy!

About

Hi, I'm Lonneke your typical travelnerd. Making photographs became a passion of mine after I started travelling, by making photographs I wanted to capture all the beautiful thiongs and places I encountered to share with the people around me. I got a lot of positive feedback from them and figured why not try to sell them to support my travels so I can keep on delivering beautiful photographs for those that are interested.

I sincerely believe that photography is an art form that can't be cheated on. It all depends on the setting, the feeling and the focus used when making a photograph. Please enjoy and if you want to know more about me or my content don't hesitate to contact me or follow me on social media.

Equipment

Warning, Tech talk! So some photographers among us might like to know what camera I'm using and for the not so experienced ones among us, You're going to love this. You see I only have one lens and a relatively small body. The Camera I use is an Olympus OM-D E-M10 Mark III.

The only lens (for now) I use with it is a m.zuiko 14-150 mm pro lens. This lens is considered as an allround lens, meaning you can shoot pretty much anything with it. From macro to landscape and everything in between. The quality is amazing and you don't need to know all the ins and outs of a manual camera yet. So for you people out there trying to get the hang of photography and wanting to learn how to shoot manually (raw), I can't recommend this camera enough.
